# Objection Taxonomy v1

This taxonomy categorizes call transcripts into measurable objection types.

## Categories

### 1. Intro Pitch
Initial introduction or opening value statement.

### 2. Intro Smalltalk
Light conversational openers before business discussion.

### 3. Busy / Callback
Prospect requests callback or indicates timing issue.

### 4. Not Interested (Polite)
Prospect declines politely without deeper objection.

### 5. Gatekeeper / Wrong Person
Reached someone who is not decision maker.

### 6. Pricing Concern
Prospect expresses cost sensitivity or payment concerns.

### 7. Payment Processing Objection
Specific objection related to current payment setup.
